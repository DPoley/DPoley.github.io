Nikola Jokic

Why Nikola Jokic? Nikola Jokic "the Joker" has faced adversity during his time in the NBA. He is now the back-to-back MVP for the Denver Nuggets and is hoping to win a championship in Denver.

The challenges Nikola Jokic has faced leading up to the NBA, and his successes in the NBA.

  • Grew up in Sombor, Serbia in a small two bedroom apartment with his parents and two brothers
  • Drafted in the second round 41st pick overall
  • Won his first MVP in the 2020-2021 season
  • Won his first MVP in the 2021-2022 season
  • Jokic was awarded a massive contract extension making him one of the highest paid NBA athletes.

Nikola Jokic is the first ever second round pick to win two MVP awards in the NBA. Although when he was drafted a Taco Bell commerical was being aired on TV, Nikola Jokic is looking to win a championship in Denver and play for the Nuggets his entire career.
